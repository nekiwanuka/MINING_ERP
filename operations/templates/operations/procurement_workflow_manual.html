Workflow Manual

Reviewed requisition to supplier purchase order

Use this process when procurement needs to review, edit, split, and send purchase orders to suppliers.

1. Review the requester requisition

Open Procurement, then choose Review / edit. Confirm the requester's items, urgency, language, and pieces. Correct descriptions or item counts before accepting.

2. Accept after review

Saving the review accepts the requisition and makes each item available for supplier purchase orders.

3. Split by item type or quantity

For each item, click Split / Create PO. Enter the quantity to order now. Use the full remaining quantity for one supplier, or enter a smaller quantity to split the item across multiple suppliers.

4. Create the supplier purchase order

Choose an existing supplier or type a new supplier name with contact, email, and phone. Confirm the item description, split quantity, amount, order date, send method, and supplier message.

5. Send to supplier

Open the purchase order detail page. Use Send email when the supplier has email, Send WhatsApp when the supplier has a phone number, or Print PO for a hard copy.

6. Continue until complete

If an item was split, repeat purchase order creation for the remaining quantity. Once every item quantity is fully ordered, the requisition becomes Purchased.
